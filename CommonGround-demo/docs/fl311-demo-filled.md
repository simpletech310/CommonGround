## 12. Additional custody provisions

**[X]** I request the following additional orders:

1. **Communication:** Both parents shall keep each other informed of their current address, phone numbers, and email. Changes must be communicated within 48 hours.

2. **Medical decisions:** For non-emergency medical decisions, both parents must consult and agree. In emergencies, the parent with the child may authorize treatment and must notify the other parent within 24 hours.

3. **Education:** Both parents have the right to access school records, attend school events, and communicate with teachers. Major educational decisions (school choice, special education) require mutual agreement.

4. **Extracurricular activities:** Parents shall consult about enrolling children in activities. Activities scheduled during one parent's time require that parent's approval. Costs for mutually agreed activities shall be shared 50/50.

5. **Right of first refusal:** If either parent is unavailable for 4+ hours during their parenting time, they must first offer the time to the other parent before arranging alternative childcare.

6. **Relocation:** Either parent intending to relocate more than 50 miles from the current residence must provide 45 days written notice and seek modification of custody orders if necessary.

7. **No disparagement:** Neither parent shall make negative comments about the other parent in the presence of the children or allow others to do so.

8. **Flexibility:** Parents are encouraged to be flexible and accommodate reasonable requests for schedule changes, with the understanding that make-up time will be provided.
